Ministry of Growth and Information
==================================
URL: https://github.com/ashdnazg/LD34

This is a game created for the 34th Ludum Dare competition. (http://www.ludumdare.com/compo)

The themes for the competition were "Growing" and "Two button controls".

You play a government official in a totalitarian state.

Your job is to censor subversive radio stations.



Thanks for playing our game!
